About the Cofee Shop

Located in downtown Alura Coffee Shop brings to the market something new and a lot of good experiences. Founded by Douglas Adams in collaboration with Isaac Asimov, Alura already is a phenomenon in the city.

Our mission is: To change how our clients live and experience coffee.

We offer the most experienced baristas in town and a large range of kinds of coffee to give you a better coffee experience in your life.
